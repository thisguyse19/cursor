The Journey

15 Days in the Wild South

Fly into Hobart and loop Tasmania clockwise — convict history, pristine rainforest, glowing granite beaches, and alpine wilderness — then fly to Melbourne for the city's legendary laneways and coffee, before tackling the breathtaking Great Ocean Road.

Tasmania · Dec 7–17
✈ SIN → Hobart→ Hobart→ Bruny Is.→ Maria Is.→ Port Arthur→ Freycinet→ Bay of Fires→ Cradle Mtn→ ✈ Melbourne

Fly from Singapore into Hobart Airport, clockwise loop of Tasmania in the RAV4, then fly Hobart → Melbourne on Day 11.

Melbourne & Great Ocean Road · Dec 17–21
Melbourne→ Torquay→ Lorne→ Apollo Bay→ Cape Otway→ 12 Apostles→ ✈ Fly home

Fly in from Tasmania on Day 11, two Melbourne city days, then drive the Great Ocean Road westward for the most dramatic coastal views.

Trip Highlights
🎨
MONA, Hobart
World-class provocative art museum carved into sandstone cliffs above the Derwent River
🏖
Wineglass Bay
Consistently voted one of the world's top 10 beaches — pink granite, white sand, teal water
🦘
Cradle Mountain
Alpine wilderness with almost-guaranteed wombat sightings on the Dove Lake circuit
🪨
Bay of Fires
50km of orange-lichen granite boulders, white sand, and the clearest turquoise water in Tasmania
🌊
Twelve Apostles
Limestone stacks rising from the Southern Ocean — most dramatic at sunrise or sunset

🧍 Jordan arrives Dec 5 — two days of free & easy in Hobart before the rest of the squad lands on Dec 7. The official group trip starts Dec 7.

Flight tips: Book your Singapore → Hobart return flights and your Hobart → Melbourne flight as early as possible — December peak season prices double for late bookings. Check-in bags count separately on budget carriers. The RAV4 is picked up and dropped off at Hobart Airport — no one-way surcharge.

Accommodation

Where to Stay

All Stays

14 Nights, 7 Locations

All prices are whole-home Airbnb estimates for 4 guests in December 2026 peak season. Book Coles Bay (Freycinet) and Cradle Mountain the moment you decide — they sell out months ahead. The Melbourne stay covers Dec 17–18 (2 nights), arriving after the Hobart flight.

💡 Pro tip: Look for properties with a full kitchen to save on food costs — self-catering breakfast and a few dinners per week can save $50–80 per person per day. Supermarkets: Hobart, Launceston, St Helens, and Sheffield all have Coles, Woolworths, or IGA.
